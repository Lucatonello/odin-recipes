Noodles

a type of food made from unleavened dough that is rolled flat, cut, stretched, or extruded into long strips or strings

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to the package instructions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the pan. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the sliced bell pepper and carrot to the pan. Continue to sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan. Stir well to combine the noodles and vegetables.
  6. Add the soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well to evenly distribute the seasonings.
  7. Continue to cook for a few more minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
